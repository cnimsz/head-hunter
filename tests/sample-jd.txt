Chief Revenue Officer — TechScale (Series C SaaS, Berlin)

About us:
TechScale is a fast-growing B2B SaaS company serving the European mid-market. We've raised €45M to date from leading European VCs and are on a clear path to €50M ARR by end of 2026. We operate across DACH, the Nordics, and Benelux, with enterprise customers in fintech, insurance, and professional services.

The role:
We're hiring a Chief Revenue Officer to own the full commercial engine — sales, partnerships, revenue operations, and customer success. Reporting directly to the CEO, you will build and scale a team of 40+ and be responsible for hitting our €50M ARR target within 18 months of joining.

Key responsibilities:
- Own the full commercial P&L across new business, expansion, and renewal
- Design and operationalize the go-to-market motion for mid-market and enterprise segments
- Build and mentor a high-performing sales, CS, and partnerships team
- Partner with the CEO on fundraising and board-level commercial narrative
- Establish the revenue operations function, including CRM hygiene, forecasting rigor, and attribution

What we're looking for:
- 10+ years in commercial leadership roles at B2B SaaS companies
- Demonstrable track record scaling ARR from sub-€10M to €50M+
- Experience in European markets, ideally DACH and Nordics
- Background in PE/VC-backed environments with strong board reporting skills
- Comfort with regulated verticals (fintech, insurance) a plus
- Hands-on leadership style — you roll up your sleeves, not just delegate
- Multilingual preferred (English required, German or another European language a strong plus)

Compensation:
Competitive base, material equity, and OTE aligned to the growth plan. Full benefits package.

Location:
Berlin (hybrid, 3 days in-office). Some travel across EU markets required (~25%).

How to apply:
Submit CV and cover letter via our careers portal. We review applications on a rolling basis and move quickly for strong candidates.
